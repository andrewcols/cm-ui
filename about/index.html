About Creator Moon

CreatorMoon ($CMOON) is a utility token built on the Binance Smart Chain Network with the purpose of Supporting Aspiring Content Creators and charities around the World.

CreatorMoon uses already proven functional structure for Our Contract.

Three simple functions occur in each trade:

  • Reflection.
  • LP Acquisition.
  • Burn.

+ 60% of Tokens get burned From Newly Accumulated LP Every Week.
